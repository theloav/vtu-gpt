VTU ACADEMIC CALENDAR - SPRING SEMESTER 2024
Vel Tech University - Important Academic Dates

EXAMINATION SCHEDULE

Mid Semester Examinations:
The Mid Semester Examinations for all undergraduate and postgraduate programs will be conducted from March 15, 2024 to March 25, 2024. All students must report to their respective examination centers 30 minutes before the scheduled time.

End Semester Examinations:
End Semester Examinations are scheduled from May 10, 2024 to May 25, 2024. The detailed timetable will be published on the university website by April 20, 2024.

REGISTRATION AND DEADLINES

Course Registration Deadline:
All students must complete their course registration by February 28, 2024. Late registration will be allowed until March 5, 2024 with a penalty fee of Rs. 500.

Fee Payment Deadline:
Semester fees must be paid by March 10, 2024. Students who fail to pay fees by the deadline will not be allowed to attend examinations.

Project Submission Deadline:
Final year project submissions are due on April 30, 2024. No extensions will be granted beyond this date.

ACADEMIC HOLIDAYS

Holi Festival Holiday:
The university will remain closed on March 25, 2024 for Holi celebrations. Classes will resume on March 26, 2024.

Good Friday Holiday:
Good Friday holiday is declared on March 29, 2024. All academic activities are suspended for the day.

Summer Break:
Summer vacation begins on June 1, 2024 and classes for the next semester will commence on July 15, 2024.

WORKSHOPS AND EVENTS

Artificial Intelligence Workshop:
A three-day workshop on "AI and Machine Learning in Industry" will be conducted from April 5, 2024 to April 7, 2024. Registration is mandatory for all final year students.

Career Fair Event:
The annual career fair is scheduled for April 12, 2024 from 9:00 AM to 5:00 PM in the main auditorium. Over 50 companies are expected to participate.

Technical Symposium:
The department technical symposium "TechFest 2024" will be held on April 20, 2024. Students can register for various competitions until April 15, 2024.

RESULT DECLARATIONS

Internal Assessment Results:
Internal assessment results for all subjects will be published on March 30, 2024. Students can view their results through the student portal.

Semester Results:
Final semester results will be declared on June 15, 2024. Degree certificates will be available for collection from June 20, 2024.

IMPORTANT NOTICES

Library Extended Hours:
During examination periods (March 15-25 and May 10-25), the library will remain open until 11:00 PM to facilitate student preparation.

Hostel Checkout:
Students residing in hostels must complete checkout procedures by May 30, 2024. Room keys must be submitted to the hostel warden.

For any queries regarding the academic calendar, please contact the Academic Office at academic@veltech.edu.in or call +91-44-12345678.

This calendar is subject to change. Students are advised to check the university website regularly for updates.
