VTU ACADEMIC CALENDAR - MID YEAR UPDATES (JUL-SEP 2025)
Vel Tech University - Important Academic Dates

JULY 2025 (PAST)

Classes Resume for Monsoon Semester:
Classes for the Monsoon semester will commence on July 14, 2025. All students must attend the first lecture.

Late Registration Window:
Late course registration will be open from July 16, 2025 to July 18, 2025 with a penalty fee.

Library Orientation Sessions:
Library orientation for first-year students will be conducted on July 20, 2025 in two slots: 10:00 AM and 2:00 PM.

AUGUST 2025 (CURRENT)

Independence Day Holiday:
The university will remain closed on August 15, 2025 in observance of Independence Day.

Add/Drop Deadline:
The add/drop window for courses closes on August 20, 2025. No changes will be accepted after this date.

Department Orientation Week:
Department-level orientation activities will be conducted from August 11, 2025 to August 13, 2025.

Lab Safety Training:
Mandatory laboratory safety training sessions will be held on August 22, 2025.

Practical Examinations (Phase I):
Phase I practical examinations are scheduled from August 25, 2025 to August 30, 2025.

SEPTEMBER 2025 (UPCOMING)

Mid-Semester Examinations:
Mid-semester exams are scheduled from September 22, 2025 to September 26, 2025. The detailed timetable will be released by September 10, 2025.

IEEE TechConnect 2025:
The annual technical conference "IEEE TechConnect 2025" will be held on September 12, 2025.

Research Workshop on AI Safety:
A one-day research workshop on "AI Safety and Responsible Innovation" is scheduled for September 14, 2025.

Ganesh Chaturthi Holiday:
University holiday is declared on September 8, 2025.

Project Proposal Submission Deadline:
Final-year project proposal submissions are due on September 5, 2025.
